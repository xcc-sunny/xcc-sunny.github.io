Introduction

Mingyang Xu is currently a researcher at Ritsumeikan University, Japan. His research interests include human-robot interaction, human augmentation, bioinspired robotics, and mechatronics design.


In 2023, he received his Master degree in Information, Production and Systems Engineering from Waseda University, Japan. His bachelor degree was obtained in Mechanical Engineering with Excellent Graduate Honor in 2019 from Beihang University, China.


For more information, please refer to the latest CV.
